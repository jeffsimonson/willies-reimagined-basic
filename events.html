Getting Beyond Winey Series

Our introductory wine courses are for anyone who doesn't know much about wines, but would like to learn.

Get Beyond Winey 101: How to Be a Wine Snob:

For people brand new to wine, those who want to impress a date, or for those who need to take out that business client that knows wine but you don’t know a thing. Learn what really matters and impress your friends with your vast wine knowledge! This is intended to be a humorous take on wine snobs.

Get Beyond Winey 102: Everything That Really Matters About Wine But You Were Afraid to Ask:

If you have tasted wine before and the only adjective you have to describe it is “winey” this class is for you. This is about taste, not terroir. Learn the Get Beyond Winey System of tasting wine and the secret element you need in order to truly taste it.
